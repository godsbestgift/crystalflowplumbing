Local History

Newmanstown sits along Route 419 in Lebanon County, in the same Tulpehocken Valley farmland that stretches west from Womelsdorf. Like much of this corridor, it was settled by German and Swiss farming families in the 1700s and 1800s, and a good share of the borough's older homes reflect that long agricultural history.

Newmanstown is a short drive out Route 419 from our Womelsdorf base, comfortably inside our same-day response range.

The mix here runs from older borough homes near the center of town to farm properties on well and septic further out. We clear and repair drain lines across all of it, and if a clog keeps coming back, we'll run a camera to find the actual cause instead of just clearing the symptom.
