Questions

What homeowners ask before booking a repair.

Can you install a fixture I bought myself?
Yes. Bring home the faucet, toilet, or vanity you like and we'll install it clean. We'll tell you upfront if the one you picked has a known problem, before it goes in the wall.
How do you decide between repairing and replacing?
We tell you both prices and what each buys you. If a repair only postpones the same failure, we say so; if the fixture has years left, we don't push a replacement you don't need. Your call either way.
Is the work under warranty?
Yes, our workmanship carries a 1 year warranty. If something we installed or repaired acts up, call and we make it right.
Do I get a price before you start?
Always. You hear the number before any work begins, no surprise charges once the job's underway.
